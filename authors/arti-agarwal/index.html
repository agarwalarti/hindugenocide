Arti Agarwal

Founder/ Researcher

Arti Agarwal is a pioneer in documenting the persecution of Hindus, working tirelessly to raise awareness on the subject since 2018. She is a researcher and an alum of IIT Roorkee, MIT and on track to complete her PhD at IIT Kanpur. Her dedication towards building a record of countless hate crimes against Hindus stems from the rapid decline in vulnerable Hindu populations in many parts of the world, and a silent genocide of Hindus that goes unreported and unrecognized.

The work of documentation is not an attempt on her part to "rake up the past" but to correct understanding of history, as Hindus. Many Hindus face horrendous crimes and carry that trauma for the rest of their lives. While documentation cannot change that, this work is a small attempt at preventing further such cases of persecution and targeting of Hindus on the basis of their religion and beliefs.

Arti's focus has, since the beginning, been on giving a "home" to all facts, details and information regarding persecution of Hindus worldwide. It's her belief that this is the only way the world can wake up to the staggering numbers of Hindus facing unspeakable crimes.
