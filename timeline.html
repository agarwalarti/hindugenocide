Points to Note

This documentation is exhaustive but not yet complete, and is indicative of the long drawn struggle faced by Hindus. All references are given at the bottom of the page, and where linked to an article, within the article. You can read the articles with more details by clicking on "Read More". There are several other terrorist attacks and incidents where Hindus have been murdered, but since it is not yet officially established that Hindus were selectively targeted, we have not included those in this list.

Apart from the items in the list, there are a zillion other incidents where the casualties and rapes are in fewer numbers. Though these have been omitted in the timeline view, they will be expanded upon in the yearly analysis of compiled data (for eg, the 2020 database), and are added in the final figure shown on the homepage.

If some important massacre or incident is missing from the timeline, please send us information at: hello@hindugenocide.com
