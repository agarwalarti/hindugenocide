About this page

This page tracks attacks on Hindu temples specifically — separate from, but related to, the human toll documented on the main timeline. An entry can appear on both pages: where an incident involved both casualties and temple destruction, it carries both sets of fields, and shows up on each timeline with the figures relevant to that page.

If you know of a temple that was destroyed, vandalized, desecrated, or converted and isn't yet reflected here, please send us information at: hindugenocide@proton.me
