Privacy Policy

This page explains what happens to your information when you visit hindugenocide.com, and how to get in touch if you'd like to share information for this archive.

Information we collect directly

This website has no contact forms, comment sections, or account registration, and it does not set any cookies of its own. The only personal information we receive is whatever you choose to send us by email — see "Submitting information" below.

Submitting information

If you know of an incident, source, or correction that should be added to this archive, you can send it to us at hindugenocide@proton.me. Information submitted this way may be reviewed, verified where possible, and added to the website for educational and historical-record purposes. Whoever submits information is responsible for ensuring they have the right to share it and that it is accurate to the best of their knowledge. If you'd like something you've sent us amended or removed, just email us and we'll address it.

Cookies and analytics

This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand which pages are visited and how the site is performing. Cloudflare Web Analytics is designed to be privacy-friendly: it does not use cookies, local storage, or any other persistent identifiers, and it does not track individual visitors across sites or over time. It reports aggregate figures only — for example, how many people visited a page, and roughly which country, device or browser they used.

Beyond this, the website itself does not set any cookies or use any other tracking tools. As our hosting and content-delivery provider, Cloudflare may set a small number of technical cookies as part of operating its network — for example, for security or load-balancing purposes. These are set by Cloudflare's infrastructure rather than chosen by us; you can read more in Cloudflare's cookie policy.

Other services this website relies on

Like almost all websites, loading a page on hindugenocide.com causes your browser to make a few requests beyond our own files:

  • Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com).
  • Charts on the Timeline and Temples pages are rendered using a JavaScript library loaded from a public CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com).
  • This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which may keep standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and page requested) for security and reliability, as is normal for any website host. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for how Cloudflare itself handles this.

These services may receive your IP address as a normal part of how the web works, in order to deliver the requested file to your browser. This website does not separately collect, store, or share that information.

Embedded content

Some articles — particularly in the Talks section — may include embedded videos from YouTube. If you play one of these, YouTube's own privacy policy applies to that interaction, in the same way as it would if you visited YouTube directly.

What third parties we receive data from

We receive information about Hindu persecution from any source that has already made it available in the public domain, or from individuals willing to share lawfully obtained information for inclusion in this archive (typically by email, as above). Anyone submitting information takes responsibility for its accuracy and for having the right to share it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time, particularly if the website's features change. The most recent version will always be the one published here.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, or about anything you've submitted to us, email hindugenocide@proton.me.
